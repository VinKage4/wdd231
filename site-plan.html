Site Plan

PokéBiz TCG Hub

Planning document: name, purpose, scenarios, color schema, typography, and wireframes for the Pokémon TCG mini-site.

Site Name

PokéBiz TCG Hub — concise, memorable, and clearly tied to the Pokémon Trading Card Game. “Hub” signals a central place for players and store owners.

Optional domain idea: pokebizhub.org (availability not required for this class).

Site Purpose

The site serves as a community hub for Pokémon TCG players and local businesses. It provides:

  • Upcoming events and league meetups.
  • Weather snapshot for event planning.
  • A directory of stores/services (grading, live-code cards, sealed products).
  • Spotlight ads for supporting Gold/Silver members.

Scenarios

  • Player: “Where can I find a league night near me this week?”
  • Collector: “Which local shops sell sealed product and do trade nights?”
  • Store owner: “How do I join the chamber and get a homepage spotlight?”

Color Schema

BG — #eef7ee
Page background & subtle panels
Surface — #ffffff
Cards, header, footer
Ink — #111827
Primary text
Accent — #0ea5e9
Links, interactive accents
Accent-2 — #22c55e
Primary buttons (e.g., “Apply”)
Bar — #2f2f2f
Card title bars & badges

Typography

Headings — Poppins (600–700)

Used for <h1>, <h2>, and section titles to provide clear hierarchy.

Body — Inter (400–700)

Used for paragraphs, lists, inputs, buttons; optimized for readability across screen sizes.

Wireframes (Home Page)

The wireframes reflect the layout you’re already building: hero + CTA, events/weather/forecast, and 2–3 rotating spotlight cards.
